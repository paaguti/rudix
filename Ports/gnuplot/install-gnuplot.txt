generate PDFs correctly

cairo-1.16.0.pkg
fontconfig-2.13.1.pkg
freetype-2.9.1.pkg
fribidi-1.0.5.pkg
glib-2.54.3.pkg
libcerf-1.10.pkg
libjpeg-9c.pkg
libpng-1.6.14.pkg
libtiff-4.0.10.pkg
ossp-uuid-1.6.2.pkg
pango-1.42.4.pkg
pixman-0.34.0.pkg
poppler-0.72.0.pkg

Plus extra dependecies that may arise for one of those packages
